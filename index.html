Tanja Aitamurto Somebody

I'm a postdoctoral fellow at Stanford University. In my work I examine new technologies for informing, empowering, and connecting people.

New media — augmented reality or applications for harnessing collective intelligence — are vehicles to achieve these goals. We need to know how these technologies impact people’s behavior and thus also society so that we can rein them for social good.
