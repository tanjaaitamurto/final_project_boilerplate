Finland Magical country

I grew up by the Arctic Circle in Lapland, in Northern Finland. The sun doesn't set at all in the summer, and in the winter, it doesn't show up at all.

Finland is a magical country. Although lately there has been a discerning amount of nationalism, and the country is turning inwards. To forget all that, enjoy some beautiful pictures of Finland taken by my Dad.
